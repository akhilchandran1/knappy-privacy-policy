Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 31/03/2026

Introduction

Knappy (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal and financial data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the Knappy mobile application.

Current Version (Offline Functionality)

Knappy is currently designed as an offline-first application. Users can download and use the app without creating an account or connecting to the internet.

All data entered into the app, including financial records and business information, is stored locally on the user’s device. Knappy does not transmit, collect, or store this data on external servers in the current version.

Users have full control over their data at all times.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial records from your app usage
  • Location data
  • Device tracking data

Future Updates (Cloud Features)

Future versions of Knappy may introduce optional features such as cloud backup, account login, and subscription-based services.

These features will be entirely optional. Users who prefer to continue using the app offline will be able to do so without being required to create an account.

Any data collection related to cloud features will only occur with user consent and will be clearly explained at that time.

Data Security

We prioritize your data security.

  • All current data remains on-device
  • No external transmission in current version
  • Future versions will follow industry-standard security practices

Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell user data
  • Share data with third parties
  • Use data for advertising

Knappy does not require users to sign up or provide personal information to use the app in its current version.

User Control

Users can:

  • Delete data by uninstalling the app
  • Manage backups manually if used

Third-Party Services

Knappy currently does not use:

  • Analytics tools
  • Advertising services
  • External tracking

Children’s Privacy

Knappy is not intended for use by children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

Updates will be reflected with a revised effective date.
